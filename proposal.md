# Project Proposal: Predicting Heart Disease

* **Are you working alone or with someone else?** I am working alone.
* **What is your plan for working on this?** I will perform exploratory data analysis, handle missing values, train a classification model, and evaluate its performance. 
* **How much time do you plan to dedicate to working on your project?** I plan to dedicate 10 hours a week to completing this project.
* **What dataset are you going to use?** I am using the [UCI Heart Disease Dataset](https://uci.edu). It tracks clinical measurements like cholesterol and resting blood pressure to determine if a patient has heart disease.
* **Null Hypothesis ($H_0$):** For the dataset of heart disease patients, there will be no difference between the average cholesterol levels of patients who develop heart disease and those who do not.
* **Alternative Hypothesis ($H_a$):** For the dataset of heart disease patients, there will be a difference in the average cholesterol levels of patients who develop heart disease and those who do not.
* **What models do you plan to apply to your data first?** I plan to apply Logistic Regression to my data first because its feature coefficients make it highly interpretable for testing my hypothesis on cholesterol.
